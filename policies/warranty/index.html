What This Warranty Covers

Subject to inspection and approval, the warranty may cover:

  • Manufacturing defects in metal or craftsmanship
  • Structural defects arising from workmanship errors

All repairs under warranty are carried out at our discretion, based on inspection findings.

What This Warranty Does NOT Cover (Exclusions)

This limited warranty does not apply to damage or defects resulting from:

  • Normal wear and tear
  • Daily usage marks, scratches, dents, or surface changes
  • Improper use, mishandling, or negligence
  • Accidental damage or impact
  • Exposure to chemicals, perfumes, cosmetics, cleaning agents, or moisture
  • Water damage (unless explicitly stated as water-resistant)
  • Improper storage conditions
  • Alterations, resizing, polishing, or repairs by third parties
  • Loss or dislodgement of stones, parts, clasps, or fittings due to wear or negligence
  • Fading or changes in finish due to regular use

These exclusions are standard for jewellery products and are disclosed to ensure informed purchasing and responsible product care.

Inspection & Decision Authority

  • All warranty claims are subject to thorough inspection by authorised personnel of Sapna Shri Jewellers.

    • Nature of the issue

  • The decision regarding warranty applicability and repair feasibility shall be made solely at our discretion, based on inspection results.

This process ensures fair assessment, consistency with industry standards, and compliance with applicable consumer protection laws.

Repair Process & Timelines

  • Once approved, warranty repairs will be carried out within a reasonable timeframe, depending on:

  • Repair timelines are indicative and not guaranteed.

Transit, Shipping & Handling Costs

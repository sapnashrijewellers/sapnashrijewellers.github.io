- Certain jewellery products are **crafted on a made-to-order basis** to ensure precision, quality finishing, and optimal craftsmanship.
- Due to inherent **manufacturing tolerances** involved in jewellery production—including casting, polishing, stone setting, and finishing—the final weight of such products may vary slightly from the weight displayed on the website.
- The **actual delivered weight may vary by up to ±0.1 gram** from the mentioned weight.
- This variation is considered **normal and industry-accepted**, and does not impact the purity, quality, or intrinsic value of the jewellery.
- The weight displayed on the website should be treated as an **approximate reference** for made-to-order items.
- For ready-stock items, the mentioned weight represents the actual product weight unless otherwise specified.
- Final invoicing, valuation, and billing are always based on the **actual finished weight** of the product.

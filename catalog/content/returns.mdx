## Product Acceptance at Delivery

- The customer or their **authorised representative** is permitted to accept delivery after verifying the product.
- Customers are requested to **carefully inspect the product** at the time of delivery or pickup.

---

## Returns Policy

- As a general policy, **returns are not encouraged**, and customers are advised to place orders mindfully.
- However, **eligible products may be returned within 7 days** from the date of delivery, subject to the conditions mentioned below.

### Return Window
- **7 days from delivery date**
- Items must be **unused, unworn**, and in original condition.

### Non-Returnable / Non-Refundable Items
The following items are **not eligible for return or refund**, unless received in defective or incorrect condition:
- Personalised or customised jewellery
- Coins, utensils, and God idols
- Earrings (for hygiene reasons)
- Clearance or sale items
- All **international orders**

---

## Return Conditions

- Items must be returned in **original packaging** with tags intact.
- If any item (including promotional or free gifts) is missing from the return package, the refund may be reduced **up to the full MRP** of the missing item.
- Customers must **self-ship** the product back after approval.

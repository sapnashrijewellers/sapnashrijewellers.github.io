Return & Exchange Policy

Sapna Shri Jewellers believes in transparency and fair dealing. Please read this Return, Exchange, and Refund Policy carefully before placing an order, as jewellery purchases involve precious materials, customised handling, and strict quality controls.

Effective Date: October 29, 2025
Last Updated: January 21, 2026

Product Acceptance at Delivery

  • The customer or their authorised representative is permitted to accept delivery on their behalf.
  • Customers are required to inspect the package and product carefully at the time of delivery or store pickup.
  • Acceptance of delivery or pickup is deemed confirmation that:
    • The package was received in good condition
    • The product matches the order placed
    • There is no visible damage or discrepancy

Any concerns must be raised immediately as outlined below.

Returns Policy

Jewellery involves precious metals and hygiene-sensitive handling. Therefore:

  • Returns are not encouraged and customers are advised to place orders after careful consideration.
  • However, eligible products may be returned within 7 days from the date of delivery, subject to the conditions stated in this policy.

Approval of returns is at the sole discretion of Sapna Shri Jewellers, based on verification and compliance with return conditions.

Return Window

  • Returns must be initiated within 7 calendar days from the date of delivery.
  • Items must be:
    • Unused
    • Unworn
    • Free from damage
    • In original condition as delivered

Requests raised after the return window will not be entertained.

Non-Returnable / Non-Refundable Items

The following categories are strictly non-returnable and non-refundable, except in cases of verified defect or incorrect dispatch:

  • Personalised or customised jewellery
  • Made-to-order items
  • Coins, silver utensils, and God idols
  • Earrings (for hygiene and safety reasons)
  • Clearance, discounted, or sale items
  • Gift cards or promotional items
  • All international orders

Return Conditions & Eligibility

To be eligible for return:

  • The product must be returned in original packaging, including tags, certificates, invoices, and any complimentary items.
  • Missing items (including promotional or free gifts) may result in:

  • Risk of loss or damage during return transit remains with the customer.

Sapna Shri Jewellers reserves the right to reject returns that do not meet inspection or quality standards.

Exchange Policy

  • Exchanges are permitted within 7 days from the date of delivery, subject to eligibility.
  • Products may be exchanged only for items of equal or higher value.
  • Any price difference must be paid by the customer at the time of exchange.
  • Exchange items are dispatched only after the original product is received, inspected, and approved.

Availability of exchange items is subject to current stock and production timelines.
